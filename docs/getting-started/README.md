## What is SearchBox?

SearchBox is a **self-hosted, local-first document search engine** that lets you:

- 🔍 Search across PDFs, Word docs, Excel, images, and more
- 🔒 Store sensitive documents in an encrypted vault
- 📁 Index entire folders automatically
- 🤖 Get AI-powered summaries (optional)
- 👥 Share search with your team (Cloud version)

Everything runs on **your infrastructure** — nothing leaves your computer.

---

## Prerequisites

### Required
- **Docker** and **Docker Compose**
- Documents to index (PDFs, Word docs, etc.)

### Optional
- **Ollama** for AI summaries
- **qBittorrent** for torrent integration
